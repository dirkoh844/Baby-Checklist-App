Cloudflare Worker (recommended) — free, nothing expires, and the list cannot be read without the token. Deploy worker/baby-list-worker.js, then paste the workers.dev URL plus your TOKEN.

JSONBin.io — free account, private bins, no auto-deletion. Paste https://api.jsonbin.io/v3/b/YOUR_BIN_ID and an Access Key with read + update, not your Master Key. The free allowance is 10,000 requests total, so it fits light use.
