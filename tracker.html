How the tracker syncs, archives & warns

Connected phones merge individual entries instead of replacing the whole log. Entries older than 30 days move to a local archive included in Save-to-file backups. Newborn count prompts appear only through day 28 and only after you mark the log complete; feeding method, gestational age, growth, and the clinician's plan still take priority.
