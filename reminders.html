Your reminders

How delivery works

With Push on: arrives even when the app is closed

A repo workflow checks every 15 minutes and sends what's due in this phone's timezone. Push needs Cloud sync (set up in Settings) and, on iPhone, the app added to the Home Screen.

Without push: only while the app is open

Reminders fire while this app is open or installed and running, with a 3-hour catch-up when reopened.

Times are per phone. Toggling or retiming a reminder updates your push schedule automatically.
