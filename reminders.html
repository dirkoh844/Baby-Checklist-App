How delivery works

With Push on: arrives even when the app is closed

A repository workflow is scheduled about every five minutes and checks what is due in this phone's named timezone. Hosted schedules can start late or occasionally be skipped, so use these as convenience nudges—not for medication, feeding, or any safety-critical deadline. Push needs private sync (set up in Settings) and, on iPhone, the app added to the Home Screen.

Without push: only while the app is open

Reminders fire while this app is open or installed and running, with a 3-hour catch-up when reopened.

Times follow this phone's timezone, including daylight-saving changes. Toggling or retiming a reminder updates the private push registry. Delivery timing is not guaranteed.
